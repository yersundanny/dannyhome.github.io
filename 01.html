What is a Rocket? 로켓은 무엇일까요?

A rocket (from Italian rocchetto "bobbin")[nb 1][1] is a missile, spacecraft, aircraft or other vehicle that obtains thrust from a rocket engine.

rocket은 이탈리아어 rocchetto "bobbin"로 부터 왔습니다. 미사일, 우주선, 항공기 혹은 "로켓엔진"으로 부터 추진력을 얻어 움직이는 탈것을 말합니다.

How Rocket Flies? 로켓은 어떻게 날아갈까요?

Rocket engines work by action and reaction and push rockets forward simply by expelling their exhaust in the opposite direction at high speed, and can therefore work in the vacuum of space.

로켓은 작용 반작용의 법칙에 의해 움직입니다. 간단히 말해 움직이고자 하는 반대 방향으로 힘을 분사하여 로켓을 밀어내는 것입니다. 때문에 진공인 우주에서도 움직일 수 있죠.

What is a 3D Printer? 3D 프린터는 무엇일까요?

Full Printed

3D printing, also known as additive manufacturing (AM), refers to processes used to create a three-dimensional object[1] in which layers of material are formed under computer control to create an object.
Objects can be of almost any shape or geometry and are produced using digital model data from a 3D model
